Introducting CourseBot

Feb 18, 2021

CourseBot let you manage courses easily.

CourseBot offers you a clean and beautiful designed Course Graph, thus you are able to check out courses by day or by week.

CourseBot will not record, obtain or disclose your personal data. The courses data will only be stored locally, and will not be transmitted to any third parties.

If you have any suggestions or complaints, please feel free to tell us.
